About Us

Siphon is founded by a team of passionate students from TU Delft from diverse backgrounds, united by a shared vision of transforming the energy landscape. Our mission is to enable reliable, sustainable energy exchanges that empower communities to take control of their energy futures.

Our Founding Story

Siphon was born out of a shared belief in the power of sustainable energy solutions. We recognized the need for innovative approaches to energy exchange, and together, we created Siphon. Our platform bridges the gap between energy demand and supply, creating a seamless experience for sustainable energy use and trading.
